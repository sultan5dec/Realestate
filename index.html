FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions asked about our
eco-friendly homes.

Here are some important questions that are frequently asked and the
answers to the questions.

Have a different question?
What makes EcoHaven Realty different from other real estate agencies?

At EcoHaven Realty, we are committed to promoting sustainability in the real estate industry. We
prioritize eco-friendly practices, such as green building techniques and energy-efficient systems, in all
of our transactions. We believe that promoting environmental responsibility is not only the right thing to
do, but it also benefits our clients by providing long-term cost savings and a healthier living
environment.

Do you only work with eco-friendly properties?

While we specialize in promoting and selling eco-friendly properties, we work with all types of
properties. Our focus is on promoting sustainable practices and educating our clients on how they can
make their properties more eco-friendly.

Can you help me find an eco-friendly property?

Absolutely! We have a database of eco-friendly properties and can work with you to find a property
that aligns with your sustainability goals. Additionally, we can provide guidance on how to make any
property more eco-friendly.

What is your process for ensuring a property is eco-friendly?

We work with builders and contractors who specialise in eco-friendly building practices. We also
conduct assessments of a property's energy and water usage, as well as its overall sustainability
features, to ensure that it aligns with our eco-friendly standards.

Are eco-friendly properties more expensive?

Not necessarily. While some eco-friendly features may require an initial investment, such as solar
panels or energy-efficient systems, they can ultimately lead to long-term cost savings on utility bills.
Additionally, there are often government incentives and tax breaks available for eco-friendly
properties and features.
